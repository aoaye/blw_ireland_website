About BLW Ireland Zone

Making An Impact; In universities across the nation and beyond...

Our Mission

BLW Ireland Zone is dedicated to building lives, transforming communities, and spreading hope throughout Ireland. We are committed to sharing the love of Christ and making a positive impact in the lives of individuals and families.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of light and hope in Ireland, creating a community where people can grow in faith, find purpose, and experience the transformative power of God's love. We envision a church that reaches beyond its walls to impact the nation.

What We Believe

  • We believe in the power of God's word to transform lives
  • We believe in the importance of community and fellowship
  • We believe in serving others with love and compassion
  • We believe in the power of prayer and worship
  • We believe in building strong families and relationships
